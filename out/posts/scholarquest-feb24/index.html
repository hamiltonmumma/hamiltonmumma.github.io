ScholarQuest Update: Submissions & Progress Live! 🎯


Big update shipped today — Phase 3 is now live with submissions, grading, and progress tracking.


What's New (Feb 24)


✅ Phase 3: Submission & Grading


**Student Submissions**

  • Submit proof of completion (text, links)
  • View submission history
  • Track status: pending AI review → guardian approval → approved

    • **AI-Assisted Grading**

  • First-pass automatic grading against custom rubrics
  • Guardian can approve or override
  • Saves parents time on routine feedback

    • **Guardian Review Flow**

  • Review AI-graded submissions
  • Approve points to award
  • Or adjust and approve

    • ✅ Phase 4: Progress Tracking (Partial)


    **Student Dashboard**

  • Points earned
  • Challenges completed
  • Current challenge status

    • **Family Leaderboard**

  • See how everyone in the family is doing
  • Competition breeds motivation!

    • **Analytics (New)**

  • Completion rates
  • Category breakdown
  • Family activity overview

  • **Reward redemption** — actual point-to-dollar conversion
  • **Streaks & achievements** — motivation boosters
